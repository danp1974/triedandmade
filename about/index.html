Why I Built This Site

I made this site mostly to help out others that are trying to repair or make something work. I have had lots of experiences from machining, air conditioning contracting, utility grid system operating, and just being a plain old person who never wants to stop learning how something is made or just to make it myself.

I have put in a lot of frustrating hours of research on several different projects where the internet just ran me through circles and I would still not find the answers I was looking for. So I put this site together to provide what I think are the most direct and simplest ways to actually build or make work whatever you're working on.
