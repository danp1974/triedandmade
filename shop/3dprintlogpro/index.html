FAQ

Questions answered.

Do I need Microsoft Excel to use this?
No. 3DPrintLogPro has a full built-in viewer — you can browse, search, edit, and manage all your prints without any external software. Excel export is an optional bonus for users who want to analyze their data in a spreadsheet.
What slicers are supported?
PrusaSlicer, OrcaSlicer, Bambu Studio, SuperSlicer, and Cura. Binary .bgcode format (PrusaSlicer 2.7+) is fully supported as well.
Can I use it on multiple computers?
Yes — each license supports up to 3 computers. Activate on your workshop PC, your desk PC, and your laptop. If you need to move a license to a new machine, deactivate it from the old one in the Settings tab.
What if I get a new computer?
Open 3DPrintLogPro on your old computer, go to Settings → Deactivate License, and that slot is freed up immediately. Then activate on your new machine. If your old machine is unavailable, email us at info@triedandmade.com and we'll sort it out.
Does it work if I'm not connected to the internet?
Yes. After activation the app runs fully offline. It does a silent background license check every 7 days and allows a 30-day grace period if you can't connect. Your print data is stored locally on your machine.
