Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Plain English summary: I don't sell your data. I collect only what's necessary to run the site and process purchases. Desktop software products (such as 3DPrintLogPro) collect a Machine ID for license activation and may monitor a local folder you choose — all data stays on your computer. If you sign up for a mailing list, I only send maker-related content and you can unsubscribe anytime.

Who we are

TriedAndMade.com is a personal website owned and operated by an individual based in Florida, United States. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@triedandmade.com

What information we collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address and name if you contact us via email or a contact form
  • Email address if you voluntarily subscribe to a mailing list
  • Purchase information (name, email, payment details) when buying digital products — note that payment details are processed by our payment provider and are not stored on our servers

Information collected automatically (website):

  • Basic analytics data such as pages visited, time spent on pages, browser type, and general geographic location (country/region level only) — collected through Cloudflare's built-in analytics, which does not use cookies or track individuals
  • IP address logs maintained by our hosting provider (Cloudflare) for security purposes

Information collected by desktop software products (e.g. 3DPrintLogPro):

Our desktop applications may collect and store the following data on your local computer for the purpose of license management and application functionality. This data is stored locally and is not transmitted to our servers except as described below.

  • Machine ID: A unique hardware identifier generated from your computer's system properties (such as computer name and hardware fingerprint). This ID is used solely for license activation and to enforce the per-machine license limit. It is transmitted to our license verification service at the time of activation and during periodic license checks. It is not used to identify you personally and is not shared with third parties.
  • Folder Watcher paths: If you enable the Folder Watcher / Auto Import feature, the application monitors a local folder on your computer that you select. The folder path you configure is stored locally in the application's settings. The application reads filenames and file contents (G-code or 3MF files) within that folder only to extract print settings for your log. This folder path and file data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party — it stays entirely on your machine.
  • Print log data: All print records, settings, photos, and notes you enter into the application are stored in a local database file on your computer. This data is never uploaded to our servers.

What we do NOT collect:

  • We do not use advertising tracking cookies
  • We do not build advertising profiles on visitors
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information
  • Desktop software does not transmit your print log data, file contents, or folder paths to our servers

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Deliver digital products you have purchased
  • Send you emails you have subscribed to receive
  • Verify and enforce software license activations
  • Monitor site performance and security
  • Improve the content and functionality of the site and software

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Email mailing list

If you choose to subscribe to our mailing list, you will receive occasional emails about new guides, new products, and maker-related content. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us at info@triedandmade.com

We will never send spam or share your email address with third parties.

Digital product purchases

When you purchase a digital product, your transaction is processed through Gumroad, our third-party payment processor. We collect your name and email address to deliver your purchase and for our records. Payment card information is handled entirely by Gumroad and is never stored on our servers. Gumroad's privacy policy applies to data they collect during checkout.

Cookies

TriedAndMade.com does not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Our hosting provider, Cloudflare, may set technical cookies necessary for security and performance. These are not used to track you across other websites.

Third-party services

This site uses the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Cloudflare — hosting, security, and basic analytics. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • GitHub — content storage. GitHub Privacy Policy
  • Gumroad — digital product sales and payment processing. Gumroad Privacy Policy

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you are subscribed to our mailing list. Purchase records are retained as required for tax and legal purposes. License activation records (including Machine ID) are retained for the duration of your active license. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

Your rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any mailing list at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@triedandmade.com

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at info@triedandmade.com
